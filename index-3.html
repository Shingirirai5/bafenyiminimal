Bafenyi - is the Setswana word for “winners”.
Our heart is to see all people win regardless of their circumstances.

Annual Reports

Who we are

We want to see the lives of people who are disadvantaged and hopeless, transformed by equipping and empowering them to become positive contributing members of society. Bafenyi Trust partners and collaborates with like minded organisations to bring hope, love and transformation to those that need it most.

1. Vision

To see people win regardless of their circumstances by bringing sustainable transformation while keeping our eyes on Jesus Christ.

2. Our Mission

To bring spiritual & social upliftment through evangelism, discipleship, life skills training and alleviation of hunger in our local community.

3. Our Story

Bafenyi Trust, founded in 2009, is an organisation passionate and driven towards community development in Ikageng, Promosa and Potchefstroom.

What we do

Atisa

We have 6 weekly discipleship groups where we minister the word of God and teach life skills to 300 adults. Our desire is to see our beneficiaries become responsible, kind, compassionate, and God-fearing individuals who will positively impact society.
